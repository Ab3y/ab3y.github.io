About Me

I'm a Senior Cloud Solutions Architect with 23+ years in IT, specializing in Cloud & AI applications within the financial services industry. My career spans the full spectrum of technology, from helpdesk support and installing server racks to networking, front and backend development, database modernization, rules engine management, process mining, and log analytics.

Along the way, I grew from individual contributor to team lead, then scrum master, and project manager, building a strong foundation in both technical delivery and people leadership. Today I focus on cloud architecture, AI agents, and AI-assisted coding. As a GitHub Copilot Adoption Lead, I champion AI-powered developer productivity, guiding teams through the adoption of modern tooling to accelerate innovation and software delivery.

Prior to financial services, I gained industry experience across Healthcare, Public Sector (Government & Education), Defense, Energy (Oil & Gas), and Event Marketing & Trade Show Exhibits.
